Terms & Refund Policy

  • Prices listed above may vary according to complexity and time required to complete them.
  • I have the right to accept or refuse any commission.
  • Full refunds are available only before the initial sketch is complete. If you request a refund after a sketch is done and before the work is finished, I will refund you 50% of the original commission price. After the complete work has been mostly completed, refunds will not be provided.
